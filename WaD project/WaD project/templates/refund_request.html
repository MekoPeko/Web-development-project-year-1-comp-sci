Refund Policy

  • Booking cancellation before 60 days of booking date does not incur cancellation charges.
  • Booking cancellation between 30 and 60 days of booking date will incur charges up to 40% of booking price.
  • Within 30 days of booking date 100% of booking price will be charged.
  • Refund requests are typically processed within 7-10 business days.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method unless otherwise specified.
  • For bookings within 24 hours of departure, refunds may not be available.
